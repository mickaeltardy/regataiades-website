Welcome to the Régataïades 2016

The biggest international rowing competition in France: 31st edition

Legendary university regatta

  • Teams from various european universities competing
  • Great test for french rowers two weeks before national university rowing championship
  • Party for all participants

Two contests in the weekend

  • Saturday 7 May 2016 : 1000m races.
  • Sunday 8 May 2016 : 500m sprint races.

Two categories available

  • Coxed eight (M8+ & W8+)
  • Quadruple scull (M4x & W4x)

Real championship contests setup

  • Starting gates and buoy lanes installation
  • Federal referees invited

Quality hosting

  • Rowing club located in the center of Nantes
  • Meals on booking
  • Officials diner and party on Saturday night
